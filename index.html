Qigong

  • Qi (Chi) ~ breath / life force
  • Gong (Gung) ~ work

Qi Gong is the practice of working with the life energy, learning how to control the flow and distribution of qi to improve the health and harmony of mind and body.

Categories of Qi Gong

  • Active Qi-Gong (dong gong)
  • Passive Qi-Gong (jing gong)

  • Eight Pieces of Brocade (Pa Tuan Chin)
  • Hsing Yi (Form and Intent)
  • Pa Kua Chang (Eight Triagrams Palm)
  • Play of the Five Beasts (Wu Chin Hsi)
  • Shao Lin Temple Style (Shao Lin Chuan)
  • The Six Syllable Secret (Liu Yin Chueh)
  • Standing Pylon (Chan Chuang)
